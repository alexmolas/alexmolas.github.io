Llibre Club — Help & Support

Getting started

  • Sign in with your Google or Apple account.
  • Complete your profile (name and date of birth).
  • Create a club or join one using an invite code from a friend.

Managing clubs

  • Organizers can add books, set the currently reading book, and manage members.
  • Any member can vote on nominated books.
  • To leave a club, go to the Members tab and tap "Leave Club".

Account

  • To log out, go to Settings > Log Out.
  • To delete your account, go to Settings > Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data.
