Llibre Club — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Llibre Club ("we", "our", "the app") is a book club organizer for local reading groups.

What we collect

  • Account information: your name, email address, and date of birth, provided during sign-up and profile setup.
  • Club activity: clubs you create or join, book nominations, votes, and membership data.
  • Authentication tokens: managed by Google or Apple sign-in. We do not store your password.

How we use it

  • To provide the core service: managing clubs, books, and memberships.
  • To display your name to other club members.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data storage

Data is stored on Supabase (hosted in the EU). Authentication is handled by Google and Apple OAuth.

Data retention and deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app (Settings > Delete Account). Upon account deletion, your profile, memberships, and votes are permanently removed.

Third-party services

  • Google Sign-In
  • Apple Sign-In
  • Supabase
